Panel S6a (trimer angle evolution vs. temperature) and panel S6b (trimer bond order evolution vs. temperature) were generated using code_trimer_angle_order_plot_vf.py and code_trimer_bond_order_plot_vf.py from local-order_metrics/, respectively.
