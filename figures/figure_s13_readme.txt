Figure S13 consists of 30 panels, one per simulation temperature (50 K to 700 K), each showing the temperature-dependent phonon dispersion of the time-averaged monoclinic structure of RuP with an inset phonon DOS. All panels were generated using 5-plot_band_structure_improved.py from the ml-phonons/ directory, reading the pre-computed band_data_{T}K.npz files. The 30 panels were assembled in Inkscape to form the complete figure.
